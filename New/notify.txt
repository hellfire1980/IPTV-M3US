[COLOR red][B]WHAT IS A BUILD?[/B][/COLOR
A Build, is a complete packaged Kodi tweaked and set up. so includes addons, extras, custom menus, custom wallpaper, one of a kind apps. etc

Basicly each Build is a new Kodi. 
Sometimes they can be good, sometimes they can be bad. 
But once you find the right type, then you'll want to create a backup and keep it..
